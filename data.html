OPEN ENERGY DATA

The Open Energy Data project is part of Department of Energy's Digital Strategy, and is intended to improve access to DOE data. The result is a data catalog that is a continually-growing list of publicly available DOE datasets.

In pursuit of its mission, DOE has generated, collected and compiled large amount of digitized data that has helped us better understand America’s energy, environmental, and nuclear challenges.
